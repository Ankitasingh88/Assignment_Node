
Capital of Sweden : 

Stockholm is the capital and largest city of Sweden, known for its stunning architecture, vibrant culture, and rich history.]

Built on 14 Islands [The city is spread across 14 islands connected by over 50 bridges, earning it the nickname "Venice of the North."]

Home to the Nobel Prize [The prestigious Nobel Prize ceremonies take place in Stockholm every year, 
                          except for the Peace Prize, which is awarded in Oslo, Norway.]

Vasa Museum [One of Stockholm's most famous attractions is the Vasa Museum, 
               which houses a well-preserved 17th-century warship that sank on its maiden voyage.]

Green and Sustainable [Stockholm is known for its commitment to sustainability, with clean public transportation, abundant green spaces, and eco-friendly initiatives, 
                        making it one of the world's most environmentally friendly cities.]
